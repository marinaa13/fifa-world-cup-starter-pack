Tickets

How to get tickets for the remaining FIFA World Cup 2026 matches, and what they cost.

How to buy

Tickets are sold through the official FIFA Ticket Portal. A FIFA Official Resale Platform is also available for fans who can no longer attend a match they hold tickets for. All face-value prices below carry an additional 15% service fee at checkout.

A limited Supporter Entry Tier at $60 is available for every match, including the final — but only through national football federations, for fans with a qualifying attendance history. It is not available through general sale.
